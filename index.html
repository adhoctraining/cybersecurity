Adhoc Training

Adhoc Training was created with several puposes in mind. First, it serves as a platform that allows the author to to grow and showcase offensive, defense and cyber operations body of knowledge. Second, it is a tool for others to use to develop their skills. Third, it is a post graduate project that will be used in gathering dissertation research in support of an effective way to teach cybersecurity.
